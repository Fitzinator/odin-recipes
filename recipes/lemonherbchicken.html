Lemon Herb Chicken

Description

Lemon herb chicken is a simple dish, primarily comprised of chicken, the herbs, and lemon!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Lemon
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Dried Oregano
  • Parsley (chopped)

Steps

  1. Place chicken, lemon juice, and salt in a bowl
  2. Heat olive oil, place chicken into oil. Add lemon juice, oregano, and black pepper. Cook until golden brown.
  3. Garnish with parsley
