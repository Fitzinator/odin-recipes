Mac and Cheese

Description

Homemade macaroni and cheese is a simple but delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain them, and transfer to a pot
  2. Combine ingredients into cheese sauce, add to noodles, and stir
  3. Add spices and bread crumbs to macaroni
  4. Bake macaroni until golden brown
